Journal Entry 1

Based on the following readings, write a reflection using your own words. What is a website anyway? Why put things on the internet? Will websites someday become obsolete? Your journal entry can look like anything you want. It may or may not include media. Use as many words as you want to get your point across.

Upload a folder to your GitHub class website. The URL should look something like: your-github-username.github.io/class-site/journal1/ Add the URL to the spreadsheet.

  • J.R. Carpenter, A Handmade Web
  • Laurel Schwulst, My Website is a Shifting House
  • Ursula K. Le Guin, A Rant on Technology
